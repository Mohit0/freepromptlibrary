How it works

  1. 1

    Tap the button above

    Opens the GitHub submission form on your phone or laptop.

  2. 2

    Fill in & attach media

    Enter your prompt details and attach your image or video using the paperclip button.

  3. 3

    Submit the issue

    Automation opens a pull request. You review and merge when ready.

Accepted formats

  • Images: jpg, png, webp, gif, svg
  • Videos: mp4, webm, mov

Before you submit

  • Use the exact prompt that produced the output
  • Attach one image or video per submission
  • Make sure you have rights to share the media
